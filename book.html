About the Book

Most professionals are more impressive
than they appear

The gap isn’t competence — it’s charisma. And charisma, despite what most people assume, is not a personality trait you either have or lack. It is a measurable skill with identifiable components that can be learned, practised, and made your own.

“Charisma isn’t a gift. It’s a skill. And like any skill, it can be learned, refined, and made your own.”

Open-Source Charisma is built around a simple but powerful model: the Charisma Quadrant. It maps every communicator across two dimensions — Confidence and Social Skills — and places them in one of four types.

The Four Types

Higher Confidence · Lower Social Skills
The Occasionally Overconfident

Sharp and capable. The missing piece is connection. Add empathy and the confidence lands very differently.

Higher Confidence · Higher Social Skills
The Courteous Charismatic

Competent and connected. The challenge is keeping it real as the stakes rise.

Lower Confidence · Lower Social Skills
The Unfortunately Invisible

Talented but overlooked. The gap isn’t ability — it’s visibility.

Lower Confidence · Higher Social Skills
The Unknowingly Influential

Warm and well-liked. The only thing underselling you is you.

The model works because it is honest. Most charisma books tell you to be more present, more engaging, more likeable. This one shows you exactly where you are, why, and what to do about it — in practical, specific steps drawn from thirty years of working with senior leaders, sales teams, and client-facing professionals at FTSE 100 companies.

The result is 125 small, high-impact actions organised around your type. Not a transformation programme. Not a personality overhaul.

A map, and the moves that matter most from where you are standing right now.
